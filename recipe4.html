Undhiyu

Prep Time: 20 minutes
Cook Time: 15 minutes
Difficulty: Easy
Servings: 4 people

Ingredients

Vegetables:

  • 1 cup purple yam (kand), cubed
  • 1 cup raw banana, cubed
  • 1 cup small potatoes
  • 6-8 baby brinjals (eggplant)
  • 8-10 valor beans (flat beans)
  • 1 cup green peas
  • 8-10 methi muthia (fenugreek dumplings)

For Masala Paste:

  • 1 cup fresh coconut, grated
  • 1/2 cup roasted peanuts
  • 2 tbsp sesame seeds
  • 1 tbsp coriander seeds
  • 1 tbsp cumin seeds
  • 4-5 garlic cloves
  • 2 green chilies
  • 1 inch ginger
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste

Other:

  • 1/4 cup oil
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • Pinch of asafoetida
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Step-by-Step Instructions

Step 1: Prep Vegetables

Cube all vegetables into similar sizes. Make small slits in baby brinjals. String the valor beans if needed. Keep everything ready before starting.

Step 2: Make Masala

Roast sesame seeds, coriander seeds, and cumin seeds lightly. Grind coconut, peanuts, roasted seeds, garlic, ginger, green chilies, turmeric, and salt into a coarse paste. Add little water if needed.

Step 3: Tempering

Heat oil in a heavy bottom pot. Add mustard seeds and asafoetida. Let mustard seeds splutter.

Step 4: Add Vegetables

Add harder vegetables first - purple yam, raw banana, potatoes. Stir fry for 5 minutes. Then add brinjals, valor beans, and peas.

Step 5: Add Masala

Add the ground masala paste. Mix everything well. Add salt if needed. Add 1/2 cup water. Mix gently.

Step 6: Cook

Cover and cook on low heat for 30-35 minutes. Stir occasionally. Add methi muthia in the last 10 minutes. Cook till all vegetables are tender and masala is well combined.

Step 7: Serve

Garnish with fresh coriander. Serve hot with puri or chapati. Traditionally eaten with jalebi!

Pro Tips

  • This is a winter special dish
  • All vegetables should be cut in similar sizes
  • Fresh coconut gives best flavor
  • Don't add too much water - vegetables release moisture
  • Cook on low heat for even cooking
  • Tastes even better the next day
  • Can skip vegetables you don't have
