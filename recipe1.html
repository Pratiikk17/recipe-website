Khaman Dhokla

Prep Time: 20 minutes
Cook Time: 15 minutes
Difficulty: Easy
Servings: 4 people

Ingredients

For Dhokla Batter:

  • 1 cup besan (gram flour)
  • 2 tbsp sooji (semolina)
  • 3/4 cup water
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp ginger-green chili paste
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tsp Eno fruit salt

For Tempering:

  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1 tsp sesame seeds
  • 4-5 curry leaves
  • 2 green chilies, slit
  • Pinch of asafoetida (hing)
  • 2 tbsp water

Garnish:

  • Fresh coriander leaves, chopped
  • Fresh coconut, grated

Step-by-Step Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Batter

Mix besan and sooji in a bowl. Add water gradually to make a smooth batter. Make sure there are no lumps. The consistency should be like pancake batter - not too thick, not too thin.

Step 2: Add Flavors

Add turmeric, ginger-chili paste, lemon juice, sugar, and salt. Mix well and let it rest for 10 minutes. This helps the sooji absorb water properly.

Step 3: Steam Setup

Grease a dhokla plate or any flat dish with oil. Put water in a steamer or pressure cooker (without whistle) and let it heat up.

Step 4: Add Eno

Just before steaming, add Eno fruit salt to the batter and mix gently in one direction. The batter will become fluffy and bubbly. Work quickly!

Step 5: Steam the Dhokla

Pour batter immediately into the greased plate. Steam for 12-15 minutes on medium heat. Check with a toothpick - if it comes out clean, dhokla is done.

Step 6: Tempering

Heat oil in a small pan. Add mustard seeds, let them splutter. Add sesame seeds, curry leaves, green chilies, and hing. Add 2 tbsp water carefully (it will splutter!).

Step 7: Finish

Pour the tempering evenly over the steamed dhokla. Let it absorb for 2 minutes. Cut into pieces, garnish with coriander and coconut.

Pro Tips

  • Don't overmix after adding Eno - mix gently
  • Steam on medium heat, not high
  • If dhokla is hard, your batter was too thick
  • Always serve warm for best taste
  • Pairs perfectly with green chutney
