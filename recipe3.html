Khandvi

Prep Time: 20 minutes
Cook Time: 15 minutes
Difficulty: Easy
Servings: 4 people

Ingredients

For Batter:

  • 1 cup besan (gram flour)
  • 1 cup buttermilk or yogurt (diluted)
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp ginger-green chili paste
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/2 cup water

For Tempering:

  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1 tsp sesame seeds
  • 8-10 curry leaves
  • Pinch of asafoetida

Garnish:

  • Fresh coriander, chopped
  • Fresh coconut, grated

Step-by-Step Instructions

Step 1: Prepare Batter

Mix besan with buttermilk to make a smooth paste. Add turmeric, ginger-chili paste, salt, and water. Mix very well till completely smooth - no lumps!

Step 2: Cook the Batter

Pour mixture in a heavy bottom pan. Cook on low flame, stirring continuously. This is important - don't stop stirring! Cook for 8-10 minutes till it thickens.

Step 3: Check Consistency

To test if it's ready, spread a small amount on the back of a greased plate. If it peels off easily without breaking, it's done. If it breaks, cook for 2 more minutes.

Step 4: Spread

Grease the back of steel plates or flat surface with oil. Quickly spread hot mixture thinly and evenly. Work fast before it cools down!

Step 5: Roll

Let it cool for 2-3 minutes till slightly warm (not completely cold). Cut into strips. Roll each strip tightly from one end. This needs practice!

Step 6: Tempering

Heat oil in a small pan. Add mustard seeds, let them splutter. Add sesame seeds, curry leaves, and hing. Pour over rolled khandvi.

Step 7: Garnish

Sprinkle chopped coriander and grated coconut on top. Serve immediately or refrigerate.

Pro Tips

  • This recipe needs practice - don't give up!
  • Stir continuously while cooking
  • The consistency test is very important
  • Spread quickly before it cools
  • Roll while still slightly warm
  • If first batch doesn't work, adjust next one
  • Best eaten fresh but can be refrigerated
